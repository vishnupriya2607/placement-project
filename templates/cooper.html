Company Description

Company Name: Mr. Cooper

Website: www.mrcooper.com

Mr. Cooper Group Inc. provides quality servicing, origination, and transaction-based services related principally to single-family residences throughout the United States. The company operates under its primary brands, Mr. Cooper® and Xome®. Mr. Cooper is one of the largest home loan servicers in the country.

Headquartered in Lewisville, Texas, Mr. Cooper employs over 8,000 employees and has a servicing portfolio in excess of $660 billion. The company is a leading provider in the residential mortgage market in the US.
